### L4 vfs verification (AbstractMachine native backend, WSL2 Ubuntu 24.04, gcc 13.3) ###
Build:  cd oslabs/user && make   &&   cd oslabs/kernel && make ARCH=native
Run:    smp=<N> ./build/kernel-native

vfs: mount table + path resolution (namei) over three file systems:
  ramfs  at /      real read/write in-memory files & dirs; holds /bin, /tmp
  devfs  at /dev   /dev/zero /dev/null /dev/random /dev/tty
  procfs at /proc  /proc/meminfo /proc/uptime /proc/self (generated on read)
Wired under the L3 syscalls open/read/write/close/lseek/fstat/dup/mkdir/link/
unlink/chdir; fork() dups the fd table, exit() closes it, exec() loads /bin/<p>.
